Protected Area Issues

What is a Conservation Area?

Conservation Area is an area of land that is protected and that cannot be built on or used for certain purposes.

    Importance of Conservation Area
  • Essential for biodiversity conservation
  • Provides habitat and protection from hunting for threatened and endangered species
  • Protection helps maintain ecological processes that cannot survive in most intensely managed landscapes and seascapes.
